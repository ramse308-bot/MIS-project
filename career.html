Working at Nike would allow me to combine my passion for athletics with my creative vision in a way that few other companies could offer.

  • Cinematic Visual Style: My videography has a cinematic quality that matches Nike's brand identity. I understand composition, color grading, pacing, and how to create visuals that inspire and motivate.
